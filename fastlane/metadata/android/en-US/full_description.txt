<b>Features</b>
<ul>
    <li>Discover FreedomBox servers on the network</li>
    <li>Display a list of installed services on the selected FreedomBox</li>
    <li>Launch Android apps and web applications for available services</li>
    <li>Save a FreedomBox and connect to it from anywhere</li>
</ul>

<b>Planned Features</b>
<ul>
    <li>Save credentials for a FreedomBox and automatically login</li>
    <li>Installation help guide for various devices</li>
    <li>Automatically configure an application with the saved credentials of the FreedomBox</li>
</ul>
